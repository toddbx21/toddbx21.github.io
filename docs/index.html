cultivate a healthy social environment and encourage a willingness to learn, with respect and communication
with the peers a student shares an environment with.

Research Interests

My main area of interest in biology research is with the plasticity of diapause in the Fall Field and Sand Field Cricket.
In simpler terms, the question I would like to answer is as follows:
Is the time of pause in development within the two cricket species able to be changed with the environment,
or is there something within the genome of the crickets that sets an amount of time that the pause in development must have?
A broader impact that can come from this research is directly related to climate change.
If the environment influences the two species development rates into hatching from the eggs,
then contemporary golbal climate change may cause a problem with ecological structures with the same or similar crickets as a primary consumer.
